【托福】综合写作模板

1. 反驳模版

Both the reading and the lecture are talking about topic, but the speaker pointed out that opinion, which disagrees with the standpoint that opinion.

The reading first claims that argument. However, the instructor objects it with a new proof. The professor says that ... because .... That is why the opinion of the reading is challenged heavily.

Furthermore, the reading concieves that argument. In contray, the professor asserts that argument, He believes that ....

Finally, the professor in the listening material, maintains that argument since ..., while the reading claims that argument. Therefore, the reading passage has been contradicted again.

Therefore the speaker departs totally from the opinoin on the topics made in the reading.

2. 赞同模版

Both the reading and the lecture are talking about topic, and the speaker agrees with the perspective made in the reading.

The reading first claims that argument. Similarly, the instructor supports it with a piece of new evidence. The professor says argument because .... That is how the opinion of the reading is supported heavily.

Furthermore, the reading concieves that argument. Likewise, the professor asserts that argument, which sustains the reading as well. He believes that ....

Finally, the professor in the listening material maintains that argument since ..., just as it is said in the reading that .... Therefore, the reading passage has been popped up again.

Therefore, the speaker agrees totally with the opinion on the topics made in the reading.
